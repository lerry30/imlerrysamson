About Me

Discovering the world of programming in 2018 during my senior high school years ignited a fascination within me. I started building simple applications using C programming and found immense joy in learning and creating. This passion led me to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, specializing in web systems technology. Throughout my studies, I honed my skills as an application developer.


During my college days, I encountered the challenge of data loss due to a corrupted flash drive. This experience motivated me to develop my own recovery software using Python, utilizing file signatures to retrieve specific file types.


To me, software development is about building solutions that enhance the quality of life and improve efficiency. I believe in leveraging technology to help businesses manage crucial data, track profits, expenses, employee salaries, inventory, and more. My ultimate goal is to become a Senior Software Developer, leading a team to create impactful and meaningful solutions for everyone.
